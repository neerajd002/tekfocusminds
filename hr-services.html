  • Permanent Staffing-

    We ease the permanent staffing for our clients by following the below practices-

    • Acquiring and assessing candidates to create a curated candidate pool
    • Verification of educational background, employment history, and skills
    • Coordination and execution of interviews, both online and in person
    • Conducting reference checks
    • Overseeing background checks
    • Presenting job offers and handling salary negotiations
  • Outsourcing(RPO)-

    A full-fledged Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) collaboration provides both day-to-day operational management and high-level strategic direction throughout the entire recruitment journey, spanning from talent advisory to assistance during onboarding. These offerings are generally supported by an adaptable Applicant Tracking System (ATS). All candidates identified and recruited through this process are exclusively dedicated to the client and are not shared with any other organizations.

  • Executive Search-

    Highly sought-after, senior-level candidates stand apart from the rest. They primarily consist of passive job seekers, which means you usually won't come across their resumes on job boards. Furthermore, executive talent often requires convincing assurance of a strong match with the hiring company.

    In a competitive job market, locating the ideal fit for C-suite, senior-level, or management roles is a challenge that most companies lack the resources or capabilities to handle effectively on their own. Executing an executive search can be an arduous endeavor. The process involves actively pursuing candidates based on their skills, knowledge, and responsibilities, along with crafting compelling job descriptions, all as part of a thorough process to ultimately select top-tier talent for their C-level positions.
